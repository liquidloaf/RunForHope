ABOUT RUN FOR HOPE SINGAPORE

What is Run for Hope?

Run for Hope (RFH) is a marathon run that is hosted by Four Seasons Hotel as well as the National Cancer Centre Singapore (NCSS). This run is to help raise cancer-related awareness, as well as the support for cancer research. This run has different categories, from 3.5km to 10km, to cater to people of all ages. This year, the run will be held on the 17th of Febuary , along the routes of the Singapore Sports Hub, OCBC Square.

In Singapore

RFH is the single largest run in aid of cancer research in Singapore, since 1993. Four Seasons Hotel partnered with NCCS to support for critical, life-saving research at the NCSS center. This run is a non-competitive, leisure run, to allow people of all ages to join this worth cause.

NCC Research Funds Objectives

Advancement funds to enhance research relted capabilities, hardware, tools and processes

Seeding funds to aid scientist to emerge with new research projects. This allow the scientist to build tract record and to be able to apply for competitive grants from government agencies

Bridging funds when the need arises, to enable these scientist to work with productive momentum.

Why should you be a part of Run For Hope?

This can be one of the many ways in which you could show your support for cancer-research and care for the patients. More benefits such as keeping fit, having fun with families and friends, as well as to destress are one of the key benefits of this run too. Moreover, there are different categories to meet the different age groups of people joining the race. Moeover, the organisers will donate $5 from each registration fee to the NCC's cancer research fund.
